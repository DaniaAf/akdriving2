ABOUT US

AK DRIVING PARIS, now 10 years old, has earned a high standing reputation in the world of transport and concierge services due to its professionalism and the quality and diversity of its services.

Whether it is for a simple ride, an airport transfer, a long-distance trip, or a car at your disposal, you will be provided with your own private driver in a top of the range vehicle. AK DRIVING PARIS offers you a bespoke service, accompanying you in all your projects.

For many years AK DRIVING PARIS has been present at prestigious international events such as the Cannes Film Festival, the Paris Fashion Weeks, the ‘Salon du Bourget’… but also at professional and private events such as photoshoots, roadshows, seminars, weddings…
Thanks to its culture of efficiency and reactivity, AK DRIVING PARIS knows how to respond to and satisfy all of your needs.
